# WegwieselSync — Connect IQ Store (English)

## App name
WegwieselSync

## Short description (max 100 chars)
Load Wegwiesel routes directly onto your Edge via Bluetooth — no Connect-Sync detour.

## Description
WegwieselSync accepts six-character share codes from the Wegwiesel app and pulls the matching route straight onto your Edge over Bluetooth — like Komoot, but without the Connect-Sync detour.

How it works:
1. Build a route in the Wegwiesel planner (app or wegwiesel.app) and tap "Send to Garmin".
2. Enter the six-character code that appears on your phone here on the Edge.
3. The track lands as a FIT course in your Edge's Courses menu — ready to ride.

What Wegwiesel is: a lean, ad-free route planner for cycling, hiking and driving. Profiles for road, gravel, trekking, MTB, hiking and car routing, alternative routes, elevation profile and surface-type colored map. No account, no cloud, no data harvesting.

You'll need:
• Wegwiesel on iPhone or Android (free)
• Edge with an active Bluetooth link to the Wegwiesel app
• This Connect IQ app on your Edge

Privacy:
• The app talks only to wegwiesel.app to fetch the FIT file for your code
• No tracking pixels, no analytics, no ads
• Codes are valid for 24 hours and deleted server-side after download

Supported devices: Edge 530, 540, 550, 830, 840, 850, 1030, 1030 Plus, 1030 Bontrager, 1040, 1050, MTB, Explore 2.

Wegwiesel and the sync-code server are open-source projects by a cyclist. Feedback to info@thomas-peterson.de.
